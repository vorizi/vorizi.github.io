Community Guidelines

These Community Guidelines are applicable for users of Vorizi public rooms.

Respect the rules: You must not violate our Terms of Service or your local laws.

Be a nice person: Do not harass, bully, or annoy/irritate our other users.

Choose your words: Vorizi public rooms have some children under 18. No swearing please.

We're not buying: Don't advertise/sell your stuff on Vorizi.

The right age: If you're under 18, ask your parents first. If you're under 13, go away.

A ban is a ban: Don't try to get past our blocker if you got banned.
