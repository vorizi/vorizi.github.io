  • Why can't I see new messages?

    Check your internet connection and reload the page. If you find a bug, please report it to us via email - vorizi@outlook.com.

  • Usage

  • Am I allowed to use a fake name?

    You are. As long as the name isn't offensive to other users, we won't take any action against you.

  • Can I join a random room?

    Vorizi doesn't allow you to join random rooms. Unless you were invited to the room by someone, you are not allowed to join it.

  • Privacy

  • Can Vorizi see my chats?

    Vorizi can access your chats if we need to due to a legal obligation or for user investigation. But the team won't look at your chats for any other reason. All of your chats are completely private and invite-only.

  • Will my chats show up on Google?

    Your chats showing up on Google isn't because of Vorizi. If you put your link on a third party website or blog, and that site gets indexed by Google, your chats will show up on the search engine. There are measures you can take to get your personal information off Google.

  • Can others see my chat?

    Don't worry, unless you've shared your link with a person/group, nobody can access your chat. Vorizi is secure since we use a 10-digit random number in Chat ID's and because of our invite-only feature.
